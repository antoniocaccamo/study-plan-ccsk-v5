== CCSK v5 Certification Exam: 12-Week Daily Study Checklist
:sectanchors:
:sectids:
:sectnums: |,all|
:seclinks:

:toc: auto

A comprehensive day-by-day guide to mastering all 12 CSA Security
Guidance v5 domains for the CCSK certification exam.

'''''

=== How to Use This Study Plan

* Study Monday–Friday, rest on weekends. Each day requires 2–2.5 hours
of focused study.
* Complete all `Study Focus' and `Activities' before moving to the next
day.
* Use the `Key Resources' to access specific materials for each day.
* Track your progress by checking off days as you complete them.
* If a day feels rushed, take more time — mastery matters more than
schedule adherence.
* The `Exam Tip' for each day highlights concepts frequently tested on
the CCSK.
* Week 11–12 are intensive review and practice. Don’t skip them.
* Use official CSA sample questions and practice exams. These are the
most accurate preparation.

'''''

=== General Study Tips

* The CCSK is open-book: CSA Guidance v5 PDF + CCM v4 PDF are allowed.
No internet. Bookmark key sections before the exam.
* *Shared Responsibility Model* is foundational. Master it for every
cloud service type (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) before moving forward.
* The *data lifecycle* (Create → Store → Use → Share → Archive →
Destroy) appears in multiple domains. Learn it cold.
* *Cloud Controls Matrix (CCM)* is your reference tool. Get comfortable
navigating it early.
* *Scenario-based questions* dominate the exam. Practice applying
concepts to real-world situations.
* Identify weak domains early (Week 3-4) and allocate extra review time.
* Form or join a study group. Explaining concepts to others reinforces
your understanding.
* Avoid cramming. Consistent daily study for 12 weeks beats frantic
last-minute prep.
* CCSK is not about memorization—it’s about understanding cloud security
principles and their application.
